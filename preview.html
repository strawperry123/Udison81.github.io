Company Profile / 公司簡介

Reliable sourcing for allocated, obsolete, and difficult electronic components. 專注供應配額料、停產料與難尋電子零件。

Udison Trading Co., Ltd. is an independent stockist and distributor serving the electronics industry with ICs, transistors, diodes, TRIACs, SCRs, DIACs, capacitors, tuners, and related components.

發迪企業有限公司為獨立庫存商與電子零件代理供應商，服務項目包含 IC、電晶體、二極體、TRIAC、SCR、DIAC、電容、調諧器與相關電子元件。

The company maintains stock for obsolete, allocated, and hard-to-find items, along with excess consigned inventories for manufacturers worldwide.

公司備有停產料、配額料、難尋料與製造商委託之過剩庫存，協助全球客戶穩定取得所需料件。
